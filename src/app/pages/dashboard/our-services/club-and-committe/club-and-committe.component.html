Clubs and Committees

The GIET Library supports various clubs and committees that foster intellectual growth, community engagement, and leadership development. These groups provide students with opportunities to collaborate, share knowledge, and participate in meaningful activities.

Book Club

The Book Club meets monthly to discuss selected readings, share insights, and engage in lively discussions. Whether you are an avid reader or just looking to explore new genres, the Book Club welcomes you.

  • Meeting Time: First Wednesday of every month at 5 PM
  • Location: Library Conference Room
  • Contact: bookclub@giet.edu

Research Committee

The Research Committee promotes academic research and scholarship among students and faculty. The committee organizes workshops, seminars, and conferences to enhance research skills and provide a platform for presenting research findings.

  • Meeting Time: Last Friday of every month at 3 PM
  • Location: Research Lab 2
  • Contact: researchcommittee@giet.edu

Community Service Club

The Community Service Club engages students in volunteer work and community service projects. The club aims to make a positive impact on society and encourages students to give back to the community.

  • Meeting Time: Second Saturday of every month at 10 AM
  • Location: Community Hall
  • Contact: communityservice@giet.edu

For more information about our clubs and committees or to join, please contact the respective club/committee or the library staff at info@giet.edu.
