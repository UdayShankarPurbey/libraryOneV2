Privacy Policy

The Gandhi Institute for Education and Technology (GIET) Library is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

a. We collect personal information that you provide to us when you register for a library membership, such as your name, address, email address, and phone number.
b. We may collect information about your use of library services, including borrowed materials, attendance at library events, and usage of library computers.

2. Use of Information

a. We use your personal information to provide and manage library services.
b. We may use your information to communicate with you about library events, services, and updates.
c. We use aggregated, non-identifiable information to improve library services and for statistical analysis.

3. Information Sharing

a. We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.
b. We may share your information with third parties only with your consent or as required by law.
c. We may share aggregated, non-identifiable information with partners and sponsors for the purpose of improving library services.

4. Data Security

a. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
b. We regularly review our data collection, storage, and processing practices to ensure the security of your information.

5. Your Rights

a. You have the right to access and update your personal information held by the library.
b. You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
c. You may opt-out of receiving communications from the library at any time.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

GIET Library reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on the library's website and will be effective immediately.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the library staff for assistance.
