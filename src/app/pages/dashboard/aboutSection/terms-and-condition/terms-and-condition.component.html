Terms and Conditions

Welcome to the Gandhi Institute for Education and Technology (GIET) Library. By using our library services, you Have to comply with the following terms and conditions:

1. Library Membership

a. Membership is open to all students, faculty, and staff of GIET.
b. Members must present a valid GIET ID card to access library services.
c. Membership is non-transferable.

2. Borrowing Materials

a. Books and other materials may be borrowed for a specified period.
b. Borrowed materials must be returned by the due date.
c. Members are responsible for any loss or damage to borrowed materials.

3. Library Conduct

a. Silence must be maintained in the library.
b. Mobile phones must be on silent mode inside the library.
c. Eating and drinking are not allowed in the library.

4. Use of Library Computers

a. Library computers are for academic use only.
b. Unauthorized access to library computer systems is prohibited.
c. Downloading inappropriate or illegal content is strictly forbidden.

5. Fines and Penalties

a. Fines will be levied for overdue materials.
b. Members will be charged for any damage or loss of library materials.
c. Persistent violation of library rules may result in suspension of library privileges.

6. Changes to Terms and Conditions

GIET Library reserves the right to change these terms and conditions at any time. Changes will be posted on the library's website and will be effective immediately.

If you have any questions or concerns about these terms and conditions, please contact the library staff for assistance.
