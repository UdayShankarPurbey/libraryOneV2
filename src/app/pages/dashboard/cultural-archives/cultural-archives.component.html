Gandhi Institute For Education and Technology(GIET)

Welcome to the GIET Central Library, your gateway to a world of knowledge and inspiration. With over 34,000 books, 559 educational CDs/DVDs, and access to 165 journals, we offer a multidisciplinary platform for learning and research. Our state-of-the-art facilities include air-conditioned reading rooms and 24*7 Wi-Fi access. Join our vibrant community of learners and thinkers, where ideas flourish, and innovation thrives. Explore, discover, and grow with us at the forefront of academic excellence. Welcome to your home for endless possibilities.
